Lifi Technology

"Lifi technology," or Light Fidelity, utilizes visible light communication (VLC) to transmit data wirelessly. It employs LED lights to send data through light waves, offering high-speed internet connectivity without the need for traditional Wi-Fi. This innovative technology holds promise for various applications, including indoor navigation, smart lighting systems, and secure data transfer in environments where radio frequency interference is a concern.
